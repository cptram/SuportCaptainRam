Second Fleet
Academy

The founding crew learned everything through trial and fire. The Academy exists so you don't have to. Every recruit who walks in walks out mission-ready.

01
ORIENTATION
Org structure, ranks, divisions, rules of conduct, Discord setup, and how Standing Points work. Delivered via #academy-orientation or a live session with a Lieutenant.
REQUIRED — ALL RECRUITS
02
DIVISION FOUNDATIONS
Division-specific training covering core skills, callouts, protocols, and your first real missions. Choose your lane: SHIELD, HAUL, SCOUT, or FORGE.
DIVISION-SPECIFIC
03
LIVE OPS QUAL
Join a live operation as support crew alongside a Specialist mentor. Real missions, real stakes. Your mentor files your readiness report in #academy-graduation-log.
LIVE SESSION
04
GRADUATION
Your Division Head officially promotes you to Crew Member. Standing Points applied. Name posted in #academy-graduation-log. You are now part of the Second Fleet.
